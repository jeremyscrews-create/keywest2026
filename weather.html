March Weather Tips
Best month to visit: March is peak season in Key West for good reason — warm, dry, and breezy. You're unlikely to see rain for more than a quick passing shower.
Sunscreen is serious business: The Florida Keys sit at 24°N latitude with intense UV. SPF 50 every morning, even on cloudy days. Reapply at the beach and on the boat.
The wind is your friend on the cruise: SE trades at 8–12 mph make for ideal sailing conditions and keep the boat cool. Bring a light layer for the return leg as it can feel cooler on the water after sunset.
Sunset times: Sunset on March 19 is ~7:31 PM, March 20 is ~7:32 PM, March 21 is ~7:33 PM. Mallory Square performers start setting up around 5:30 PM. The 6–8 PM cruise catches the full golden hour.
Humidity: March humidity averages 70–75% — noticeable but not oppressive like summer. Evenings are genuinely comfortable, especially with the sea breeze.
