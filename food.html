Your Confirmed Restaurants
🌊
Thu Dinner
Louie's Backyard
700 Waddell Ave · Oceanfront
One of the most beautiful dining settings in all of Key West — right on the water. Known for fresh local seafood, creative preparations, and stunning sunset views.
🍽️ Order: Fresh catch of the day, ceviche, anything with local fish
🌮
Fri Dinner
Lolita's Coastal Cocina
1 Duval St · Formal Night
Creative coastal Mexican cuisine with stunning views. Dressy attire — this is the fancy night of the trip. Known for elevated takes on Mexican classics with fresh local seafood.
🍽️ Order: Shrimp ceviche, house margarita, fresh fish tacos

What to Drink in Key West
🍹
Classic
Rum Runner
Everywhere — it's the Key West cocktail
The official drink of Key West. A tropical blend of rum, banana liqueur, blackberry brandy, grenadine, and OJ. Frozen or on the rocks. You must have at least one.
🏆 Best at: Sunset Watersports cruise bar
☕
Morning
Cuban Coffee
Cuban Coffee Queen — multiple locations
Strong, sweet espresso pulled Cuban-style. Get a café con leche with coffee ice cubes on a hot day. A Key West morning ritual and absolute must before any beach day.
☕ Get an iced café con leche — trust us
🍺
Daytime
Frozen Margarita
Lolita's (Friday), or anywhere on Duval
Duval Street is lined with frozen drink windows. Walk and sip — it's basically encouraged. The house margarita at Lolita's is specifically worth trying.
🍹 Try the house margarita at Lolita's on Friday
🥂
Brunch
Bottomless Mimosas
Blue Heaven, Bagatelle, Hot Tin Roof
Key West brunch spots do mimosas right. Hot Tin Roof has a legendary Bloody Mary bar alongside bottomless mimosas. Perfect fuel for a Saturday on the golf cart.
🥂 Hot Tin Roof: unlimited Bloody Marys too
🏆
Key West Food Classics
🍋
Icon
Key Lime Pie
Blue Heaven, Kermit's Key Lime Shoppe
The real thing — tart, creamy, on a graham cracker crust. Try it frozen on a stick dipped in chocolate at Kermit's. Blue Heaven's version is legendary. Don't leave without trying both.
🍋 Kermit's frozen key lime pie on a stick is life-changing
🐚
Snack
Conch Fritters
Schooner Wharf, Sunset Pier, most bars
Deep-fried conch in a seasoned batter — the quintessential Key West snack. Crispy outside, tender inside. Best enjoyed with a cold drink at a waterfront bar post-cruise.
🐚 Best at Schooner Wharf Bar after the cruise
🦞
Splurge
Lobster Benedict
Blue Heaven, Hot Tin Roof
The Key West upgrade on eggs benedict — local Florida lobster instead of Canadian bacon. A brunch dish that justifies the Saturday golf cart trip to get there.
🦞 Blue Heaven's version is the most famous on the island
🐟
Local
Fresh Mahi / Yellowtail
Any seafood restaurant
Fish doesn't get fresher. Local fishermen supply the restaurants daily. Blackened mahi or grilled yellowtail snapper are the Key West standards. If it's on the menu, order it.
🐟 Always ask what was caught today
🌯
Lunch
Cuban Sandwich
Any Cuban café on Duval or nearby
Key West has a deep Cuban heritage and the sandwiches show it — pressed Cuban bread, mojo pork, ham, Swiss, pickles, mustard. Perfect Duval Street walking lunch.
🌯 Pair with a Cuban coffee for the full experience
🎸
Bar Food
Smokin' Tuna Nachos
Smokin' Tuna Saloon · off Duval
The tuna nachos at Smokin' Tuna are legendary among locals. Pile of crispy chips topped with fresh tuna, avocado, and a kick of jalapeño. Perfect with a cold beer during the live music.
🎸 Go for the music, stay for the nachos
🎸
Bars & Live Music Spots
🦜
Best Local Bar
Green Parrot Bar
601 Whitehead St · 1 block off Duval
The most authentic bar in Key West — no tourist trap energy here. Open-air dive bar where locals actually go. Pool tables, a great jukebox, and some of the best live bands on the island. Cold cheap beer.
🍺 Order: Whatever's coldest. Don't overthink it.
🎸
Live Music
Smokin' Tuna Saloon
4 Charles St · alley off Duval
Down a side alley off Duval — big outdoor space, multiple bars, massive stage. Tuna nachos are legendary. Pork tacos are excellent. One of the best afternoon live music spots on the island.
🌮 Order: Tuna nachos, pork tacos, frozen drink
🎵
Hemingway Haunt
Sloppy Joe's Bar
201 Duval St · Corner of Greene
Hemingway's legendary haunt since 1933. Live music from 10 AM every single day. Big stage, large dance floor. Worth one drink just to say you were there. Gets crowded at night — daytime visit recommended.
🍹 Order: A Sloppy Joe sandwich and a rum drink
⚓
Post-Cruise Stop
Schooner Wharf Bar
202 William St · Historic Seaport
Casual open-air waterfront bar right at the seaport — perfect landing spot after the Saturday sunset cruise. Live music, cold drinks, conch fritters, and buffalo wings. Open till 2–3 AM. Zero pretension.
🐚 Order: Conch fritters, buffalo wings, draft beer
🌅
Pre-Cruise Drinks
Sunset Pier
Ocean Key Resort · Mallory Square
Right on the harbor with unobstructed views. Perfect pre-cruise drinks Saturday afternoon before the 5:15 PM boat check-in. Lobster rolls, conch fritters, and frozen cocktails with harbor views.
🦞 Order: Lobster roll, frozen rum runner, conch fritters
☕
Sunday Morning
Two Friends Patio
512 Front St · Old Town
Live music starts at 9 AM — one of the earliest on the island. Great last Key West breakfast before the golf cart drop-off Sunday. Try the seafood omelette. The patio is shaded and relaxed.
🎵 Live music from 9 AM — perfect Sunday send-off
🥂
Saturday Brunch Contenders
🐓
Icon
Blue Heaven
729 Thomas St · Bahama Village
The most famous brunch in Key West. Shaded dirt courtyard with free-roaming roosters and cats. Lobster benedict, shrimp eggs benedict, and legendary key lime pie. Arrive early — waits hit 2 hrs on weekends.
🦞 Order: Lobster benedict, key lime pie, bottomless mimosas
🏛️
Historic Mansion
Bagatelle
115 Duval St · Historic mansion
Beautiful historic mansion on Duval, built in 1884. Brunch 7 days a week 9 AM–4 PM. Hangover Happy Hour 9–11:30 AM with $5 breakfast tapas and $5 drinks. Shaded terrace, trickling waterfall, colorful interior.
🥂 Order: Hangover Happy Hour items — best value on the island
🌊
Waterfront Splurge
Hot Tin Roof
Ocean Key Resort · Mallory Square
Sunday brunch $49/person with an incredible build-your-own Bloody Mary bar, unlimited mimosas, and 30+ menu items. Caribbean French toast, citrus beurre blanc shrimp, stunning waterfront views. Reservations recommended.
🍸 Order: The Bloody Mary bar is the main event here
☕
Old School
Pepe's Café
806 Caroline St · Oldest restaurant in KW
Open since 1909 — the oldest restaurant in Key West and still going strong. Bottomless coffee, hearty portions under the trees, homemade bread, and house hot sauces. Breakfast 7:30 AM–Noon. Cheap, honest, local.
☕ Order: Eggs any style with homemade bread and hot sauce
🥡
Quick Bites & Snacks
🍋
Must Do
Kermit's Key Lime Shoppe
200 Elizabeth St · near Duval
The frozen key lime pie on a stick dipped in chocolate is one of the best bites in Key West. Full stop. Also does key lime cookies, fudge, and whole pies to take home. Multiple Duval locations.
🍋 Get the frozen pie on a stick. Both chocolate and plain.
☕
Morning Ritual
Cuban Coffee Queen
Multiple locations · Near Duval waterfront
Strong, sweet Cuban espresso is the Key West morning ritual. Original location is a food truck near the waterfront. Get an iced café con leche with coffee ice cubes on a hot day — it'll change your life.
☕ Get the iced café con leche — coffee ice cubes are key
🌯
Walking Lunch
Cuban Sandwich (anywhere)
Any Cuban café on or near Duval
Key West has deep Cuban roots and the sandwiches show it. Pressed Cuban bread, mojo pork, ham, Swiss, pickles, mustard. Perfect Duval Street walking fuel. Pair with a Cuban coffee for the full experience.
🌯 Best paired with an iced Cuban coffee
