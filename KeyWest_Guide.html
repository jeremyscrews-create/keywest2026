🕐 Best for Friday afternoon
Tucked down a side alley off Duval. Large outdoor space, multiple bars, big stage. Great tuna nachos and pork tacos. One of the best live music spots on the island.

Green Parrot Bar
🕐 Best for Friday afternoon
The best local live music bar in Key West. Open-air dive bar one block off Duval on Whitehead. No tourist trap energy — this is where locals go. Pool tables, great jukebox when bands take a break.

Sloppy Joe's Bar
🕐 Live music from 10 AM daily
Hemingway's legendary haunt since 1933. Corner of Duval & Greene. Live music all day, big stage, large dance floor. Worth a drink just to say you did it.
